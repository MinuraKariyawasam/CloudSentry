Step 3.2: Application Monitoring Config

CloudSentry is a comprehensive cloud-native monitoring solution designed to keep your cloud deployment running smoothly. Our solution provides real-time monitoring of your Java applications, ensuring that everything is functioning optimally.

To take advantage of CloudSentry, you must follow a few simple prerequisites.

Firstly, you must add the Log Analytics Java Agent to your project. This open-source library is designed to collect and send logs to Log Analytics, making it a critical component of CloudSentry.

Secondly, you must add log statements to your Java code. This will allow the Java Agent to collect your logs and send them to Log Analytics, where they will be monitored in real-time.


Live Logs

With CloudSentry, you have the ability to check the live logs in real-time, depending on your session. This provides you with valuable insights into the behavior and performance of your deployment, allowing you to quickly identify and resolve any issues that may arise.

In addition to real-time log monitoring, CloudSentry also offers advanced log analytics capabilities. Our solution centralizes your logs, providing you with a comprehensive view of your entire cloud deployment. This makes it easier to spot trends and identify potential problems, allowing you to take proactive measures to ensure the stability and security of your cloud infrastructure.
